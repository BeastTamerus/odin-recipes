Tomato juice

Ingredients

  • 1 (46 fluid ounce) can tomato juice
  • ½ cup cider vinegar
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons prepared horseradish
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons adobo seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder

Steps

  1. Combine tomato juice, vinegar, sugar, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, adobo seasoning, and onion powder in a 2-quart container. Mix well and chill for at least 1 hour.
